IMPLEMENTATION NOTES (short, non-technical friendly)
------------------------------------------------------------

1) showWhen rules (conditional display):
- q30 shows when q29 is “yes” or “unsure”.
- q43 and q44 show when q42 is not “not relevant”.
- Any “other (write in)” option should reveal a short_text “Describe” field.

2) Answer types (answer_schema guidance):
- single_select: pick exactly one option.
- multi_select: pick any that fit (you may cap at 3–5 if desired).
- free_text: open response (suggest 1–5 sentences).
- short_text: 1 sentence or short phrase.
- number: numeric input with min/max.

3) Scales:
- q37 uses 1–10.
  - 1 = not ready at all
  - 10 = ready and at peace doing it now
(If you later add 0–100, define 0 as “no readiness at all” and 100 as “fully ready and confident.”)

4) Comfort safety:
- For sensitive questions (q26, q32, q41), ensure users can choose “prefer not to say” where appropriate, or keep “notes optional”.

5) Lite vs Full:
- Lite should feel like a warm, high-signal “check-in,” not an interrogation.
